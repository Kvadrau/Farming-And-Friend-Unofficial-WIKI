# Basic Farming

Learn the fundamentals of farming in Farming And Friends.

## Planting Crops

1. Prepare the soil by tilling the land.
2. Select seeds from your inventory.
3. Plant the seeds in the tilled soil.
4. Water the crops daily.

## Harvesting

- Crops are ready when they are fully grown.
- Use your harvesting tool to collect them.
- Store harvested crops in your barn.

## Crop Types

- **Wheat**: Easy to grow, good for beginners.
- **Corn**: Higher yield but takes longer.
- **Vegetables**: Variety of options with different growth times.
